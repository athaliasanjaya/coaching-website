Any questions? We got you.

People tend to have a lot of questions about coaching sessions. Especially if they are a first-timer. Dont worry! we have sum it up for you.

What is coaching?

Coaching is a supportive process that helps you gain clarity, self-awareness, and direction through guided reflection and conversation.

Do I need to prepare before a session?

No preparation is required. You can simply come as you are and we will explore what feels most important.

Is coaching confidential?

Yes, all coaching sessions are private and handled with care and confidentiality.

How many sessions do I need?

The number of sessions depends on your goals, with some people gaining clarity in one session and others choosing ongoing support.
